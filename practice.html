About me

Hi, I'm Taika (he/him or they/them pronouns)
CS Student and Interdisciplinary Researcher
Passion areas - Backend stack development and continuous learning

I love learning about other people's cultures and traveling to historical sites when I can. I have participated in martial arts -- shorin-ryu karate and then taekwondo -- since I was a child so one of my proudest accomplishments is keeping up with that.

I am a Student at the University of Michigan School of Information.
My entry is a little bit nontraditional. I started out at the University of Michigan studying biomedical engineering. Then I switched majors to kinesiology and neurophysiology because it was where engineering concepts are applied to improve patient outcomes.

After graduating, I applied for research positions and graduate school rather than clinical roles. I wanted to increase my technical and critical thinking skills. Through my work in research I have strengthened my passion in increasing the accessibility of resources and products to others.
